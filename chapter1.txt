Excercise 1.2

Make the following statements into questions and then answer them first in the positive and then in the negative:

1. Wǒ xǐhuan hē kāfēi
   Wǒ xǐhuan bù xǐhuan hē kāfēi?
   Wǒ xǐhuan hē kāfēi
   Wǒ bù xǐhuan hē kāfēi

2. Nǐ xièxie wǒ
   Nǐ xièxie bù xièxie wǒ?
   Wǒ xièxie nǐ
   Wǒ bù xièxie nǐ
    
3. Wáng xiānsheng qǐng wǒ zuò
   Wáng xiānsheng qǐng bù qǐng zuò?
   Wǒ qǐng zuò
   Wǒ bù qǐng zuò

4. Lǐ xiānsheng xǐhuan hē shuǐ
   Lǐ xiānsheng xǐhuan bù xǐhuan hē shuǐ?
   Wǒ xǐhuan hē shuǐ
   Wǒ bù xǐhuan hē shuǐ

5. Tā xǐhuan hē Zhōngguó chá
   Tā xǐhuan bù xǐhuan hē Zhōngguó chá?
   Wǒ xǐhuan hē Zhōngguó chá
   Wǒ bù xǐhuan hē Zhōngguó chá.
